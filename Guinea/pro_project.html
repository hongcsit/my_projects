Proposed Projects

Through the President's Malaria Initiative, the project has provided preventive and treatment interventions of malaria, along

with emergency supplies of medicine. Established health centers provided medical commodities and community volunteers

helped with distributing insecticide-treated nets for the people of Guinea. Currently, the Simandou project is underway in improving the country's

railway system. It is 650km in length and will span across the south east at the iron ore mines and in the north at the bauxite mines to the country's

deepwater ports (3). The completion of the project should increase the nation's economic foreign exchange through the exports of bauxite and iron

ore.
