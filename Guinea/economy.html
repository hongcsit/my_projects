Economy

Agriculture makes up 24% of Guinea's GDP and over 80% of the population work on farms (4). The farmers' cash

crops are fruits, such as bananas, pineapples, coconuts to peanuts and sugarcane. The livestock are raised locally and traded,

while certain species of tropical hardwood is exported. Guinea is successful in commercial fishing and their mining operations of bauxite

contributes over 78% of its GDP (2). Guinea also exports iron ore, gold, and diamonds.
